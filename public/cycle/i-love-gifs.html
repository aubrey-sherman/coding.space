I Love GIFs

(If any of the below previews are blank, right-click them and click "Reload Frame".)

In this project, we're going to use a collection of GIFS to make a sentence.

1) Go to giphy.com and search for a GIF. When you find one you like, right-click it and select "Copy Image Address."

2) From the Media Elements menu, drag an image into the page and paste your link into it.

3) From the Layout menu, drag "arrange sub-elements" into the page to center everything.

4) Continue adding GIFS until your web page is complete.
